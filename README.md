## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineer** at **[WSO2](https://wso2.com)**, passionate about **Identity & Access Management**, **API Management**, and **Cloud-Native** development. I work primarily on [Asgardeo](https://wso2.com/asgardeo/) — WSO2's IDaaS platform — and the broader WSO2 ecosystem.

- 🔭 &nbsp; Currently working on **Asgardeo** and **WSO2 Identity Server**
- 🌱 &nbsp; Exploring **Ballerina**, **gRPC**, and advanced **IAM** patterns
- 🛠️ &nbsp; Previously contributed to **WSO2 API Manager** tooling
- 🤝 &nbsp; Open to collaborating on **Open Source** and **Security** projects
- 📍 &nbsp; Based in **Sri Lanka** 🇱🇰
- 💬 &nbsp; Ask me about **IAM**, **OAuth 2.0**, **OIDC**, **FIDO2**, **API Management**, or **Ballerina**
